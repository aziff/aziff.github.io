I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at Duke University.

My research agenda includes three categories of projects related to place-based policies.

  1. The efficiency and equity consequences of interventions that are targeted based on geographic characteristics (e.g., infrastructure investment).
  2. The role of place in household outcomes (e.g., income volatility).
  3. Measurement and econometric issues related to the study of place-based policies.
